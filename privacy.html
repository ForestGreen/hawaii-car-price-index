Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Introduction

Hawaii Car Price Index ("we" or "us" or "our") operates as a free web tool to help users understand vehicle pricing in Hawaii. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Usage Data: We use Google Analytics to collect information about how you use our site, including pages visited, time spent, and general location data.
  • Email Addresses: If you opt in to our price alert service, we collect your email address to send you updates about vehicle pricing trends you're interested in.
  • Search Queries: We may collect information about car models, years, and features you search for to improve our service.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Improve and personalize your experience on our website
  • Send you price alerts and updates (only if you've opted in)
  • Understand usage patterns and optimize site performance
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support

Data Protection

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. Your information is used solely to improve our service and provide you with the features you've requested.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to track site usage. Google Analytics may collect information such as your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on our site. This data helps us understand how users interact with our website. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by using Google's opt-out tool.

Email Communications

If you subscribe to our price alert service, we will send you emails about vehicle pricing updates relevant to your interests. You can unsubscribe from these emails at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email we send.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance your experience. These cookies help us remember your preferences and understand how you use our site. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on our website and updating the "Last updated" date above.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us through our website.
