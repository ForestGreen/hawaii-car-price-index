What Drives the Premium

It's not just one factor — it's the combination of these forces.

🌊

Import Costs

Shipping used cars to Hawaii adds $1,500-$3,000 per vehicle.

🏝️

Limited Supply

Fewer cars available means less competition among dealers.

📊

High Demand

Residents need cars more than most places — limited public transit.

🔧

Parts & Service

Specialty parts must ship from mainland, increasing maintenance costs.

Methodology

Data Source: Analysis of 1,000+ used car listings tracked daily across Oahu, Maui, Big Island, and Kauai from January 2025 through March 2026.

Comparison: Hawaii prices compared against equivalent vehicles (same year, model, mileage, condition) listed on mainland marketplaces (California, Washington, Oregon).

Premium Calculation: Percentage difference = (Hawaii Price - Mainland Price) / Mainland Price × 100%

Vehicles Analyzed: Only vehicles with 50+ comparable listings to ensure statistical significance.
